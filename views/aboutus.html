About Us

Mana JRG Vaaradhi is an NGO which is mainly formed by a group of volunteers from JRG on a motto that Mother and Mother Land should not be ignored. The main objective of the NGO is to serve the JRG people via online website interface and thus work for the growth of the JRG. This is a center stage for all the people of JRG anytime, anywhere and for any type of service. This Foundation is purely formed with a motive to serve the people of our own JRG and to make everyone feel proud in belonging to JRG.
